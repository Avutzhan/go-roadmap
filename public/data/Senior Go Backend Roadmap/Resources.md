## 🏗️ System Design & Architecture
*   [**Designing Data-Intensive Applications (DDIA)**](https://www.oreilly.com/library/view/designing-data-intensive-applications/9781491903063/) — `Book` Known as the "Bible" of distributed systems. Mandatory reading for Senior/Staff engineers.
*   [**System Design Interview – An Insider's Guide**](https://bytebytego.com/books/system-design-interview-an-insiders-guide-volume-1) — `Book` Alex Xu's famous guide with structured frameworks for any SD problem.
*   [**System Design Primer**](https://github.com/donnemartin/system-design-primer) — `GitHub` Comprehensive open-source resource for diagrams, theory, and cases.
*   [**Hello Interview**](https://www.hellointerview.com/learn/system-design/master-class/introduction) — `Course` Real-world system breakdowns (TikTok, Uber, etc.) from engineers who worked there.
*   [**MIT 6.824: Distributed Systems**](https://pdos.csail.mit.edu/6.824/) — `Course` Hardcore academic course for understanding the deepest internals of distributed consensus.
*   [**ByteByteGo**](https://blog.bytebytego.com/) — `Blog` Visual breakdowns of architectural patterns and newsletter.
*   [**Codemia.io**](https://codemia.io/) — `Platform` Large collection of system design problems with structured solutions.

## 🐹 Go Language & Performance
*   [**Functional Options Pattern (Golang.cafe)**](https://golang.cafe/blog/golang-functional-options-pattern) — `Blog` A must-know pattern for flexible object configuration in Go.
*   [**Option Pattern In Go (Medium)**](https://dsysd-dev.medium.com/writing-better-code-in-go-with-the-option-pattern-bb9283131407) — `Article` Another deep dive into the Option pattern with practical examples.
*   [**Uber Go Style Guide**](https://github.com/uber-go/guide) — `Style Guide` Lessons from one of the largest Go codebases in the world.

## 🧮 Algorithms & Data Structures
*   [**LeetCode**](https://leetcode.com/) — `Platform` The standard industry practice site for coding interviews and algorithms.
*   [**NeetCode**](https://neetcode.io/) — `Platform` The best video explanations for LeetCode problems and structured roadmaps.
*   [**LeetCode Go Solutions**](https://github.com/halfrost/LeetCode-Go) — `GitHub` Massive repository of LeetCode solutions specifically in Go.

## 💾 Databases & Infrastructure
*   [**Linux Journey**](https://linuxjourney.com/) — `Tutorial` Perfect for brushing up on Linux fundamentals, CLI, and kernel basics.
*   [**SQL Zoo**](https://sqlzoo.net/) — `Interactive` Hands-on SQL practice directly in the browser.
*   [**Postgres EXPLAIN Visualizer**](https://explain.dalibo.com/) — `Tool` invaluable tool for understanding and optimizing PostgreSQL query plans.

## 🤝 Leadership & Soft Skills
*   [**The Art of the 1-on-1**](https://getlighthouse.com/blog/one-on-one-meetings-template-questions/) — `Guide` Essential reading for aspiring Tech Leads on managing people and meetings.
*   [**Amazon Leadership Principles**](https://www.amazon.jobs/content/en/our-workplace/leadership-principles) — `Principles` Standard for behavioral interviews at top-tier companies.

## 🎯 Mock Interview Platforms
*   [**interviewing.io**](https://interviewing.io/) — `Elite` Anonymous mock interviews with real FAANG engineers. Best for high-pressure practice.
*   [**Hello Interview**](https://www.hellointerview.com/) — `Elite` Premium mock interviews with Senior/Staff level coaches. Detailed feedback and strategy.
*   [**Pramp (by Exponent)**](https://www.pramp.com/) — `Peer-to-Peer` Free practice sessions with other candidates. Good for building confidence.
*   [**Exponent**](https://www.tryexponent.com/) — `Platform` Live mock interviews and detailed system design walkthroughs.
*   [**InterviewBit AI**](https://www.interviewbit.com/mock-interview/) — `AI` Interactive AI-driven mock interviews with instant performance feedback.
*   [**Layrs**](https://layrs.me/) — `AI` System design practice featuring an AI interviewer and interactive canvas.
*   [**FreeMockInterview**](https://freemockinterview.com/) — `AI` Fully free AI tool with voice support for 100+ different roles.
