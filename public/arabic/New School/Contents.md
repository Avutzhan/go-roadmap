# 🧭 Topics Roadmap (Modern + Classical Hybrid)

---

## 🔹 Stage 0 — Foundation (Pre-A1)

### 📘 Reading Preparation

* alphabet (letters)
* vowel marks (harakat)
* reading simple words

### 🎯 Goal:

* read basic Arabic without fear
* recognize letters and sounds

---

# 🟢 A1 — Basics

## 🧠 Topics:

* greetings & introductions
* names, countries, nationalities
* classroom language
* numbers & time
* basic daily verbs (eat, go, study)

---

## ⚙️ Light Sarf:

* basic verb forms (past/present recognition)
* simple pronouns (I, you, he)

---

## ⚙️ Light Nahu:

* simple nominal sentences (هذا… / أنا…)
* basic word order

---

## 🎯 What student can do:

* introduce himself
* ask simple questions
* understand basic phrases

---

# 🟢 A2 — Everyday Life

## 🧠 Topics:

* family
* home
* daily routine
* food & drinks
* shopping
* directions & places

---

## ⚙️ Light Sarf:

* past & present verb usage
* common verb patterns

---

## ⚙️ Light Nahu:

* subject–verb structure
* simple questions
* possession (my, your…)

---

## 🎯 What student can do:

* talk about daily life
* ask for things
* describe simple situations

---

# 🟡 B1 — Expanding Communication

## 🧠 Topics:

* travel
* health
* hobbies
* education
* work
* past experiences
* future plans

---

## ⚙️ Light Sarf:

* verb forms (expanded usage)
* introduction to derived forms (basic awareness)

---

## ⚙️ Light Nahu:

* past vs present sentence structures
* connectors (and, because, then)
* basic complex sentences

---

## 🎯 What student can do:

* hold conversations
* describe events
* explain preferences

---

# 🟡 B2 — Confident Communication

## 🧠 Topics:

* work & career
* problems & solutions
* opinions
* comparisons
* social issues
* technology

---

## ⚙️ Light Sarf:

* better control of verb forms
* active/passive awareness

---

## ⚙️ Light Nahu:

* structured arguments
* conditionals (if…)
* comparisons

---

## 🎯 What student can do:

* express opinions
* argue
* discuss topics in detail

---

# 🔴 C1 — Advanced Expression

## 🧠 Topics:

* abstract thinking
* culture & society
* analysis
* debate
* storytelling

---

## ⚙️ Light Sarf:

* full awareness of word patterns
* flexible usage

---

## ⚙️ Light Nahu:

* advanced sentence structures
* nuance in expression
* rhetorical clarity

---

## 🎯 What student can do:

* speak fluently
