### 🧠 Core Topics (Sarf — word formation)

* **Roots (جذر)**
  the base (usually 3 letters) from which words are built

* **Verb patterns (باب)**
  templates that change the meaning of verbs (do, cause, mutual action, etc.)

* **Past / Present tense**
  how verbs change by time and subject (I, you, he…)

* **Masdar (verbal noun)**
  the action as a noun (e.g. “reading”, “writing”)

* **Active / Passive participles**
  the doer vs the receiver of the action (writer / written)

* **Weak verbs (الأفعال المعتلة)**
  verbs with weak letters (و، ي، ا) that behave irregularly

    * Shudhur al-Dhahab

* 🔴 Advanced

    * Alfiyyah Ibn Malik
    * Sharh Ibn Aqil (full)

* ⚫ Expert

    * Mughni al-Labib

---

### 🧠 Core Topics (Nahu — sentence structure)

* **Nominal vs Verbal sentences**
  two sentence types:

    * nominal (start with a noun)
    * verbal (start with a verb)

* **Subject / Predicate (مبتدأ / خبر)**
  who/what the sentence is about + what is said about it

* **Grammatical cases (raf‘, nasb, jarr)**
  word endings that show the role in a sentence

* **Particles (حروف)**
  small words that affect meaning and grammar (in, from, to, not, etc.)

* **Objects & Complements (مفعول وغيره)**
  what the action is directed at + additional elements

* **I‘rab (الإعراب)**
  analyzing a sentence:
  identifying roles and explaining endings
